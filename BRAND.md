# QentriQ Brand Guidelines

## Logo

The logo consists of a circular **Q symbol** and a **pink accent dot**.

Two variants:

| Variant | Q symbol | Accent dot | Background |
|---------|----------|------------|------------|
| Light   | `#0B3D91` | `#FF55A3` | `#FFFFFF` |
| Dark    | `#FFFFFF` | `#FF55A3` | `#0D1117` |

## Typography

| Element | Font | Weight |
|---------|------|--------|
| Wordmark "QentriQ" | Outfit | Bold 700 |
| Tagline "Post-Quantum PKI Toolkit" | Outfit | Light 300 |

Font source: [Google Fonts — Outfit](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Outfit)

## Usage rules

- The pink accent dot is always `#FF55A3`, regardless of background.
- Never place the blue logo on a dark background — use the white variant.
- Minimum clear space around the logo: height of the accent dot on all sides.
- The site uses system fonts. Outfit is reserved for the logo and marketing materials only.
